Charges are accusations only. Tiers shown are the statutory default for the cited section. The degree actually charged in a case can be heavier or lighter based on subsections, prior offenses, and aggravating factors determined by the charging authority or the court.

Tap a section to see who is currently held under it, its plain-English meaning, the statutory max term, and where it sits on Ohio's severity ladder. {{ sections | length }} sections appear on the current roster of {{ "{:,}".format(total_roster) }} people in custody. Statutes are linked to codes.ohio.gov, the official source maintained by the Ohio Legislative Service Commission. Felony cases are heard in the Court of Common Pleas, General Division; misdemeanors in Hamilton County Municipal Court. People charged with a crime who cannot afford an attorney are entitled to one through the Hamilton County Public Defender (513-946-3700).
