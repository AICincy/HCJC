Arrest is not conviction. Everyone listed here is legally presumed innocent unless and until proven guilty in a court of law. The charges shown are accusations only and may be reduced, dismissed, or result in acquittal.

JCStream republishes public records of the Hamilton County Sheriff’s Office (HCSO) as authorized by the Ohio Public Records Act, ORC § 149.43 — derived from HCSO’s public inmate roster at hcso.org. When HCSO removes a record, it is removed here on the next update cycle; this site keeps no historical archive. JCStream is an independent, non-governmental project — not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by HCSO or any government agency.

Not a consumer reporting agency. Do not use this site for employment, housing, credit, insurance, tenant screening, or any purpose governed by the Fair Credit Reporting Act (15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq.). To report an error, or to request removal under a court order of sealing or expungement (ORC § 2953.32, as amended), open an issue at github.com/AICincy/JCStream/issues — there is never a fee, and there never will be. About JCStream — purpose, removal policy, no fees

Purpose. JCStream exists to increase public access to, and transparency of, Hamilton County’s criminal justice system. By republishing the Hamilton County Sheriff’s Office public inmate roster in a structured, searchable format, this project supports civic awareness, journalistic inquiry, court-watching, and public accountability. It does not editorialize about guilt or innocence, does not accept payment for record modification or removal, and removes records when the source agency removes them.

No fees — no exceptions. This site will never charge any person for the review, correction, or removal of their record. If you encounter any third party claiming to offer paid removal services for records on this site, that party is not affiliated with JCStream and cannot fulfill that promise. Removal requests are processed at github.com/AICincy/JCStream/issues at no cost.
