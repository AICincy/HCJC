# Register Examples Reference

## Example 1: Financial institution (Pelican CU)

Wrong register:
"I had a rough medical stretch that impacted my credit. Is there a path
for someone in my situation?"

Right register:
"My credit file was compromised by identity theft in 2023. I have filed
an FTC Identity Theft Report (Report #[number]). Three accounts currently
reporting derogatory were opened fraudulently. I am seeking a secured
credit card with a $500 deposit while the FCRA dispute process resolves
the remaining items. My income documentation and the FTC report are
available on request."

Why: Financial institutions respond to account data, statutory references,
and specific dollar amounts. Emotional framing signals "this person does
not understand the process." Factual framing signals "this person has
already done the work."

## Example 2: Federal agency (ADA Title II complaint)

Wrong register:
"I have disabilities and was denied service at a public library."

Right register:
"I am a qualified individual with a disability as defined by 42 U.S.C.
12102. On [date], the Cincinnati and Hamilton County Public Library,
a public entity within the meaning of 42 U.S.C. 12131(1), denied me
the benefit of its printing service in violation of 42 U.S.C. 12132.
The denial resulted from a policy applied without modification to a
person whose disability made compliance with that policy physically
harmful. I request investigation under 28 C.F.R. 35.170."

Why: Federal agencies process complaints against statutory elements.
Every sentence must map to a specific legal requirement. Emotional
language is filtered out during intake.

## Example 3: Peer/social media (Facebook post)

Wrong register:
"We are excited to announce the launch of aretheyinjail.com, a
transparency platform that leverages public records data to enhance
civic engagement in Hamilton County."

Right register:
"You know how the county jail website looks like the DMV designed a
wedding invitation? I fixed it. aretheyinjail.com mirrors the same
public roster, updated every 30 minutes, except you can actually
read it. Some pictures are missing because the Sheriff did not post
them. That is not a bug. That is the Sheriff."

Why: Social media requires personality, humor, and directness. Corporate
tone reads as spam. The user's natural voice is sharp, self-aware, and
carries authority through confidence rather than formality.

## Calibration principle

Register matching adjusts three things:

| Dimension | What changes | What never changes |
|---|---|---|
| Formality | Citation style, vocabulary, sentence structure | Intellectual content |
| Density | Word count, supporting detail level | Accuracy of claims |
| Tone | Warmth, humor, authority signals | The user's competence level |

The content stays at the user's level. The packaging matches the recipient.
Making the user sound less capable than they are is a skill violation.
