What JCStream will never publish

By policy, even when the underlying data may technically be public. See data & legal for the full scope statement.

  • × Identities, addresses, or phone numbers of visitors.
  • × Phone-call records, transcripts, or recordings.
  • × Commissary purchases, deposit amounts, or money transfers.
  • × Internal classification (gang affiliation, security level, behavior notes).
  • × Medical, psychiatric, or program-participation information.
  • × Identifying information about minors, victims, or witnesses.

If a record on this site is causing you harm

Records here come and go automatically: when HCSO removes a person from the public roster, that record drops from JCStream on the next sweep (about every 30 minutes). For earlier removal, for example after a court order of sealing or expungement under ORC § 2953.32, open an issue at github.com/AICincy/JCStream/issues.

There is never a fee, and there never will be.
