Visiting an inmate

How to visit someone held at the Hamilton County Justice Center

JCStream does not track visitor records. This page exists because family members and friends often arrive here looking for visitation logistics; we link out to the Hamilton County Sheriff's Office authoritative policy and do not record who visits whom. Inmate visitation is governed by HCSO and the practical details (hours, registration, video options) change without notice.

Authoritative resources

  • HCSO Justice Center services → Visitation policy, hours, video-visit registration, deposit instructions, and the "do not visit" rules around active warrants. This is the authoritative source; JCStream republishes nothing from it.
  • HCSO inmate search → The same roster JCStream mirrors, served by the Sheriff's office. Use this if you need a record straight from HCSO without JCStream's framing.
  • Hamilton County Clerk of Courts → Court schedule, case dockets, and filed documents. Public hearings are open to the public; JCStream's court calendar aggregates the next court date per inmate but is informational only.

What JCStream does not show

JCStream is a public-records mirror; it surfaces only what the Hamilton County Sheriff's Office already publishes about each inmate. By policy this site does not show, has never shown, and will never show:

  • The identities of visitors, their phone numbers, or addresses.
  • Phone-call records, recorded calls, or message transcripts.
  • Commissary purchases, deposit amounts, or money transfers.
  • Internal classification labels (gang affiliation, security level, behavioral notes).
  • Medical, psychiatric, or program-participation information.
  • Identifying information about minors, victims, or witnesses associated with any case.

Adding any of these would be a re-identification risk and is outside the project's stated transparency mission, even when underlying data may technically be public. See the data & legal page for the full scope statement and the takedown protocol.

If a record on this site is causing you harm

Records here come and go automatically: when HCSO removes a person from the public roster, that record is dropped from JCStream on the next sweep (about every 30 minutes). For earlier removal — for example after a court order of sealing or expungement under ORC § 2953.32 — open an issue at github.com/AICincy/JCStream/issues. There is never a fee, and there never will be.
