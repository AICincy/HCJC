Which Court is This?

For a full directory of every Hamilton County court (Common Pleas, Municipal, Domestic Relations, Juvenile, Probate, Appeals, and Mayor's Courts), see The Hamilton County Court System →. Quick reference below.

Common Pleas, General Div.
Felony criminal cases and civil cases over $15,000. Hamilton County Courthouse, 1000 Main Street, Room 410, Cincinnati. hamiltoncountycourts.org →
Municipal Court
Misdemeanor criminal cases, traffic cases, and civil cases under $15,000. Hamilton County Courthouse, 1000 Main Street, Room 205. Main: 513-946-5200. Clerk Criminal/Traffic: 513-946-6040.
First District Court of Appeals
Reviews appeals from Common Pleas, Municipal, and Juvenile Courts in Hamilton County. Decisions released Wednesdays and Fridays. 12th floor, William Howard Taft Law Center.
Hearings are public
Public hearings are open. The court schedule, case files, and filed documents are at courtclerk.org. Pavan Parikh, Clerk of Courts.
Scam alert: electronic monitoring "release fees" Scammers are actively impersonating Hamilton County personnel, demanding fees to release an incarcerated person to electronic GPS monitoring. Hamilton County will never require payment for electronic monitoring before release. Verify directly with the Electronic Monitoring Unit at 513-946-9800.
