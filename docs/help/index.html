Get Help in Hamilton County
Free legal aid, court services, expungement, and crisis resources

JCStream is not legal advice and not a lawyer. This page mirrors current contact information for the free Hamilton County resources most relevant to people who land on this site looking for help with the justice system. Start with the category that matches your situation.

Scam alert Scammers actively impersonate the Hamilton County Sheriff's Office, the Electronic Monitoring Unit, and the Clerk of Courts to demand release fees via Zelle, Venmo, or Cash App. Hamilton County never requires payment for electronic monitoring before release. Verify directly: Electronic Monitoring Unit 513-946-9800.

I Want a Record Sealed or Expunged

Fresh Start Expungement Clinic
Free clinic run by the Public Defender. 1st and 3rd Wednesday of each month, 1:00 PM-4:00 PM. Walk-ins are not accepted. Call 513-946-3666 between 8:30 AM and 11:30 AM on the clinic date you want to attend to claim an appointment, first come first served.
Recent expansion (3/19/2025)
Convictions for Violating a Protection Order are now eligible to be sealed. Convictions for Domestic Violence (M1 or below) and Felony Failure to Appear (F4) are now eligible to be expunged after the applicable waiting period.
If your charge was a referral to prostitution / human trafficking
Special expungement assistance is available through the Public Defender. 513-946-3700.
Why bother
A juvenile or criminal record — even decades old — can affect employment, housing, professional licensing, immigration status, and benefits eligibility.

Why This Page Exists

Many people land on JCStream because someone they love was just booked or because a record about them is showing up in public search results. The site itself is a public-records mirror and is not equipped to help. The agencies and organizations above are. This page is a curated, single-screen index of the free Hamilton County resources most relevant to people in those situations, written in plain English and kept current.

Phone numbers and links are verified from the agencies' own sites. If you find a number that has changed or an organization that no longer offers a listed service, please open an issue. There is never a fee, and there never will be.
